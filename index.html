About The Bees Knees

“We push each other to be the best. It’s not uncommon for one of us to say ‘this needs to be better, back to the drawing board” – Lead vocalist of The Bees Knees

The Bees Knees is a pop rock band originating from San Francisco, California. The band consists of a dynamic lineup featuring two lead vocalists, two guitarists, a bassist, a keyboardist, and a drummer.
